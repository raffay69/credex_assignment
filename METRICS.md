# METRICS.md

## The North Star: High-Value Leads

Our goal is to find users saving **$500/month or more** who then give us their email.

**Why:**
Traditional metrics like DAU are irrelevant for a quarterly tool. Success is measured by how many qualified conversations we initiate for Credex.

## 3 Numbers to Watch:

1. **Traffic:** How many unique visitors land on the site.
2. **Audit Completion:** What percentage of people actually finish the input form.
3. **Lead Capture:** What percentage of users with big savings actually ask for the report.

## What to Instrument First:

I would track the step-by-step funnel to see exactly where users bounce—whether it is at the tool input stage or the final email gate.

## When to Pivot:

If less than **5%** of high-saving users give us their email, the tool isn't convincing enough. This would trigger a pivot in how we display results or gated content.
